Combat

Combat Defined
    What is combat?
  • A fight or contest between individuals or a group
  • usually involves swords, fists, or guns

In DnD, The game organizes the chaos of combat into a cycle of rounds and turns. A round represents about 6 seconds in the game world. During a round, each participant in a battle takes a turn. The order of turns is determined at the beginning of a combat encounter, when everyone rolls initiative. Once everyone has taken a turn, the fight continues to the next round if neither side has defeated the other.
